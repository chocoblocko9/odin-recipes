Cheesecake

Description

The finished cheesecake on a plate with strawberries and sauce

This beautiful cheesecake uses amazing cheese and digestive biscuits to bring a wonderful flavour. Topped with halved strawberries and icing sugar, it's a wonderful treat for the family.

It only takes 30 minutes to prepare (plus overnight chilling) and serves 12-14 people

Ingredients

    For the cake

  • 250g digestive biscuits
  • 100g butter, melted
  • 1 vanilla pod
  • 600g full fat soft cheese
  • 100g icing sugar
  • 284ml pot of double cream

    • For the topping

  • 100g punnet of strawberies, halved
  • 25g icing sugar
